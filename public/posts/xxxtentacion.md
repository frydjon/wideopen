We collaborated directly with Montreality to deliver a visual that felt like X's iconic journal writings. The video compiled different moments from Montreality interviews across the years, in which his closest friends & collaborators spoke about the impact he had on them.

The video was made over 2 nights as a last minute request from Montreality. In those 48 hours, we determined the core concept & fully edited the video. At first, we simply overlayed and tracked X's original journal entries to get the base template for all the transitions. This was done using a trackpad to keep that jittery motion in his writing.

From there, we mimicked his handwriting as closely as possible to include subtitles & most importantly the words "The Final Album." Everything had to be done three to five times to create that stop motion like effect; as if his journal had come alive.
