## 1. Serve community
Every project must seek to help community.

## 2. Look for inspiration in new places
Forage for new ideas constantly in the old & the new. Flip over every rock. Don't touch a turned over rock. What you find is sacred.

## 3. Cross polinate across culture & disciplines
Anything that is fresh is connecting new dots.

## 4. Curate, curate, curate
Curation is saying no. Say no to excess. What you have left is what matters.

## 5. Simplicty
You can only achieve simplicity through attention to detail.

## 6. Experiment
Try things often, test those things. What connects will prosper.

## 7. Only do projects you would buy/want to attend
Trust your taste
